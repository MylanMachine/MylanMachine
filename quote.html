Essential Information for Accurate Quotes

Technical Drawings

Provide detailed blueprints with dimensions, tolerances, and specifications. PDF format preferred for clarity and compatibility.

Material Specifications

Specify exact material grade, hardness requirements, and any special properties needed for your application.

Quantity & Timeline

Include production quantity and required delivery date. This affects pricing and manufacturing scheduling.

Tolerance Requirements

Specify critical dimensions and their tolerance ranges. Tighter tolerances may affect pricing and lead times.

Surface Finish

Describe required surface finish, coatings, or treatments such as anodizing, plating, or heat treating.

Application Details

Understanding how the part will be used helps us recommend the best materials and manufacturing processes.

Tips for Faster Quote Processing

  • Include all relevant files in your initial email to avoid delays
  • Use clear, descriptive file names for attachments
  • Specify if this is a prototype, small batch, or production run
  • Mention any preferred communication methods or times
  • Include your company's payment terms if applicable
  • Let us know if you have existing supplier relationships we should be aware of
  • Provide context about your project timeline and flexibility
